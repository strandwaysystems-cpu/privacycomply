Key Capabilities and Privacy Compliance Solutions

Legal Document Generation

iubenda’s automated policy generation creates customized privacy policies and terms of service for your website or app. The privacy policy generator draws from a database of over 1,700 legal clauses covering common services and technologies. You select the services your site uses, and the platform assembles a compliant privacy and cookie policy tailored to your specific configuration.

The system supports multiple languages and jurisdictions, allowing you to maintain separate policies for different regions. Legal documents receive automatic updates when regulations change — when GDPR took effect in 2018, iubenda updated existing client policies without requiring manual intervention.

Cookie Consent Management

The consent management platform (CMP) provides cookie banners that collect and document user consent according to GDPR and CCPA standards. You can customize the banner’s appearance, language, and behavior to match your brand while maintaining legal compliance.

The cookie consent solution automatically scans your website to detect tracking technologies and categorizes them appropriately. It blocks non-essential cookies until users provide explicit consent, ensuring you meet strict EU requirements. iubenda stores consent records with timestamps and user preferences, creating an audit trail for regulatory authorities.

Privacy Controls and Data Protection

iubenda’s privacy management tool extends beyond cookie policies to handle broader data protection requirements. The Internal Privacy Management feature helps you document data processing activities, maintain records of processing operations, and manage data subject requests.

The platform tracks which personal data you collect, how you process it, and where it’s stored — essential for demonstrating GDPR accountability. The system includes templates for data processing agreements and helps you manage relationships with third-party processors.

Essentials Plan

The Essentials plan (€4.99/month) includes up to 20 service clauses, 25,000 monthly pageviews, automated privacy and cookie policy generation, a GDPR-compliant consent banner, and monthly website scanning. It supports one language with partial branding removal. This is the right starting point for most small business sites.

Advanced and Ultimate Plans

The Advanced plan expands to 50,000 pageviews, 30 service clauses, hourly scanning, full multi-language support, and complete branding removal. The Ultimate plan provides 150,000 monthly pageviews and unlimited service clauses. Both plans add geo-targeting, in-depth consent analytics, and consent recovery features not available on Essentials.

Compliance Toolkit and Standout Features

Cookie Banner and Consent Flow

The cookie banner automatically blocks tracking technologies until users provide explicit permission. You can customize the consent flow to match your brand while maintaining compliance with GDPR, CCPA, and other privacy regulations. The system integrates with Google Consent Mode v2 to ensure your marketing campaigns continue running without interruption while respecting user choices.

Each consent interaction gets recorded with timestamp, IP address, and specific permissions granted or denied. You can configure the banner to display different consent options based on the user’s location, automatically adjusting requirements for EU visitors versus those from other regions.

Consent Database and Data Subject Rights Management

The consent database maintains a complete audit trail of every user decision, including the specific version of your privacy policy they agreed to and when they provided consent. Data subject rights management tools help you handle access requests, deletion requests, and data portability requests through a centralized system. You can track each request from submission through completion, ensuring you meet strict regulatory deadlines.

Register of Data Processing Activities and Whistleblowing

The register of data processing activities documents what personal data you collect, why you process it, who has access, and how long you retain it — fulfilling Article 30 requirements under GDPR. The register updates automatically when you modify your privacy policy or add new services.

The whistleblowing management tool provides a secure channel for employees and external parties to report privacy violations or compliance concerns anonymously, with encrypted reports through a protected interface.

Integration, Automation, and Support

Google Consent Mode and Integrations

iubenda is an official Google CMP Partner, providing certified integration with Google Consent Mode v2. This ensures your Google Analytics and Google Ads tags adjust their behavior based on user consent choices.

The platform offers direct integrations with WordPress, Shopify, Magento, and Joomla through dedicated plugins. iubenda’s cookie scanner automatically detects tracking technologies on your website and configures the consent banner to block them until users provide permission.

Legal Team and Automated Policy Updates

iubenda employs an internal legal team that monitors regulatory changes across multiple jurisdictions. When privacy laws change, your generated policies update automatically without requiring manual intervention or additional fees. The policy generator contains over 1,700 pre-written legal clauses covering common web services.

Customer Support

Chat response times on paid plans typically arrive within one hour. Basic tier users may experience slower replies. The knowledge base covers common integration questions, and the support team can help with cookie banner customization and policy generator settings. Users consistently praise the quality of support, though the initial setup still requires some configuration work.

Pros and Cons

What we liked

  • All-in-one compliance stack. Policy generation, cookie consent, DSAR handling, and internal compliance tools under one subscription.
  • Automatic legal updates. Policies update when regulations change — no manual work required.
  • 1,700+ clause library. Covers virtually every mainstream web service and tracking technology.
  • Google-certified CMP. Official Google Consent Mode v2 partner with IAB TCF 2.2 support.
  • Multi-language support. Covers 27 languages — essential for EU-focused businesses.
  • Whistleblowing and ROPA tools. Enterprise-grade features rarely found at this price point.

Where it falls short

  • Per-site pricing. Five sites on Essentials costs €24.95/month — Termly covers unlimited sites for less.
  • Cluttered dashboard. Finding specific settings during initial setup takes longer than it should.
  • Branding below Ultimate. “Powered by iubenda” appears on all plans except the top tier.
  • Pageview overage charges. High-traffic sites can accumulate unexpected costs if not monitored.
  • No free tier worth using for business. The free plan’s 3-clause limit makes it suitable only for testing.

Who iubenda is for

  • Small to mid-sized businesses on WordPress or Shopify needing Google Consent Mode v2
  • Digital agencies managing multiple client websites (Ultimate plan)
  • E-commerce businesses needing both cookie consent and a terms & conditions generator
  • Sites with meaningful EU traffic that need multi-language policy support
  • Any business that wants policies to update automatically as regulations change

Who should look elsewhere

  • Bloggers managing 5+ sites on a tight budget → Termly flat-rate pricing
  • Sites that only need a cookie banner, no policy generation → CookieYes
  • Enterprise with custom legal requirements → OneTrust
  • US-only sites under 5 pages with no budget → Termly free tier

Frequently asked questions

How does iubenda compare to other consent management and privacy policy solutions?
iubenda combines legal document generation, cookie consent banners, and DSAR handling in one platform. Most competitors split these features. Cookiebot focuses only on consent management from $16/month but requires a separate privacy policy. Termly is cheaper for multi-site owners. What features are included in the free plan versus paid plans?
The free tier covers up to three services with iubenda branding and limited pageviews — suitable only for testing. The Essentials plan (€4.99/month) supports 20 services, 50,000 monthly pageviews, and Google Consent Mode v2. Advanced plans add multi-language support and a terms and conditions generator. How reliable is the cookie banner for GDPR and ePrivacy compliance?
The consent banner blocks third-party trackers before visitor consent and integrates with Google Consent Mode v2. It supports IAB TCF 2.2, handles GDPR, UK GDPR, CCPA/CPRA, LGPD, and FADP automatically based on visitor IP. Consent logs store proof of each visitor’s decision with timestamps and IP addresses for regulatory audits. It also meets WCAG AAA accessibility compliance under the European Accessibility Act.
How easy is iubenda to integrate with WordPress, Shopify, and Google Tag Manager?
WordPress installation takes about 30 minutes using the official plugin. Shopify, Wix, Webflow, and Squarespace use the standard script in the site header.
