Quick summary

The authoritative version is the iubenda-hosted policy linked above. Short version:

  • Strictly necessary cookies: Required for the site to work — for example, the cookie banner remembering your consent choice. Cannot be disabled without breaking site functionality.
  • Analytics cookies (Google Analytics 4): Anonymised aggregate data about which articles readers find useful. You can opt out via the cookie banner.
  • Affiliate tracking cookies: Set by third-party vendors (CookieYes, iubenda, Termly, NordLayer) when you click an affiliate link. Governed by each vendor's own cookie policy.
  • No advertising cookies: We do not run display ads. We do not set retargeting or behavioural advertising cookies.

How to change your consent

You can change or withdraw your consent at any time by clicking the privacy preferences icon in the bottom corner of any page, or by clearing cookies for privacycomply.io in your browser settings.
