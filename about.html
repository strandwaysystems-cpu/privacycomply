About PrivacyComply

📅 Updated June 5, 2026

What we do

PrivacyComply publishes independent reviews and plain-English guides for data privacy compliance tools — cookie consent banners, GDPR software, CCPA solutions, and privacy policy generators. We exist for the people who actually have to ship the website and need a clear answer faster than reading 99 articles of regulatory text.

Who runs the site

PrivacyComply is operated by Strandway Ventures, a digital holding company based in Dartmouth, Nova Scotia, Canada. Strandway Ventures runs a portfolio of internet properties across travel, niche affiliate, lead generation, and B2B SaaS review verticals. Contact the editorial team at hello@privacycomply.io.

How we test

Every tool we review goes through a documented testing process:

  1. We install the tool on a real WordPress test site with Google Analytics 4, Google Tag Manager, and a Meta Pixel running.
  2. We test it against EU, UK, California, and Brazil IP locations using a VPN.
  3. We measure: time to first live banner, Lighthouse performance impact, full regulatory coverage, hidden upgrade triggers, and how the tool behaves when a user changes consent mid-session.
  4. We run it for at least 14 days before publishing a review, and we re-test at least quarterly.

Privacy policy

Our full privacy policy is on a dedicated page: PrivacyComply Privacy Policy. Short version: standard Google Analytics 4 (IP anonymisation on), CookieYes cookie banner, no selling visitor data. Privacy requests: privacy@privacycomply.io.

Editorial corrections

If you find a factual error in any of our reviews or guides, email corrections@privacycomply.io. We re-publish with a date-stamped correction note at the top of the affected article, and we credit the reader who flagged it (unless they prefer to remain anonymous).

What we don't do

  • We do not give legal advice. Our guides explain the law in plain language so you can make informed decisions, but for a compliance audit or a specific legal situation, hire a qualified data protection lawyer in your jurisdiction.
  • We do not accept paid placement in our "best of" rankings. The ordering reflects test results, not vendor relationships.
  • We do not write fake reviews. Every product we mention is one we've used or one we've explicitly noted we're researching but have not yet tested.
