Quick summary

Even though the full policy is the authoritative version, here is the short, plain-English version:

  • Analytics: We use Google Analytics 4 (with IP anonymisation) to understand which articles readers find useful.
  • Affiliate tracking: When you click an affiliate link to CookieYes, iubenda, Termly, NordLayer, or any other partner, that vendor may set their own cookies. Their privacy policies govern that data.
  • Cookies: Our cookie banner (CookieYes) lets you opt in or out of non-essential cookies at any time.
  • No selling data: We do not sell, rent, or share visitor data with third parties for marketing purposes.
  • Your rights: You can request a copy of any data we hold on you, or ask us to delete it, by emailing privacy@privacycomply.io.
